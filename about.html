All about the Student Center!

The Student Center is designed to support learning, student organizations, and embrace campus life. Our mission is to provide welcoming, attainable spaces for students and staff.

Facilities

Study lounges, group rooms, a conference room, multi-purpose space, and an auditorium with AV support. Rooms are equipped for collaboration and presentations.

Accessibility

All public spaces follow accessibility best practices. If you need accommodations, don't hesitate to contact us via the Contact page!
