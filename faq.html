Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about hours, booking, and services? You're in the right place.

Use the Book a Room page to request a room. You will receive a confirmation if the booking is accepted.

Most rooms are free for students. However, event spaces may require departmental approval or fees.

Bookings are typically limited to 4 hours. If you need longer, contact student center staff for special consideration.

Contact campus IT for AV support — details will be included in booking confirmations.

Cancel via the confirmation email link or contact the student center desk at least 24 hours in advance.
